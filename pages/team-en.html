Team Introduction & Vision

Company Vision

Mission
By integrating AR, AI, and robotics technology, systematically solve the structural crisis in the global labor market, build a next-generation human-machine collaboration platform, and unleash the potential of "super individuals".
Vision
Become the world's leading human-machine collaboration technology provider, enabling everyone to enhance their capabilities through technology and create greater value.
Core Values
"Do Good with Good", technological equality, and empowering everyone with technology.
WarkAI was founded on a simple yet profound insight: the global labor market is facing an unprecedented structural crisis. Aging populations, skill mismatches, and employment difficulties for people with disabilities are becoming increasingly severe, while traditional solutions can no longer meet the needs. We believe that by organically combining AR, AI, and robotics technology, we can create a completely new human-machine collaboration platform that not only solves the current labor crisis but also unleashes everyone's potential and creates "super individuals".
